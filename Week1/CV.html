    • About me

    I am a Phd candidate in Department of Agricultural, Forestry and Food Sciences of University of Turin, Italy. I have been graduated from Agricultural University of Athens – Department of Natural Resources and Agricultural Engineering and I hold an MSc in Energy Systems-Renewable Energy Sources. My primary research includes operations management in bio-production, optimization of resources in agrifood and agricultural processes, energy crops assessment for biomass production and CO2 emissions evaluation in crop production systems.
